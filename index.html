About

Yokoso - The Bake House is one of the modern Japanese Bakery in Pune which offers bakery products without the addition of preservatives and additives. Breads have become integral part of the modern society lately. We have observed and understood the need of hygiene and the freshness required to such a highly demanding products of our new generation customers. Here comes the role of Yokoso- The Bake House. We are ready to serve our customers not only fresh but also hygienic breads purely handcrafted with a blend of Japanese methodology. " Yokoso" is a Japanese term which means "Welcome". We are pleased to "Yokoso" you at our shop to unleash the "Aroma of Wheat" and the real taste of bread.
